Puuppi

About the project

Puuppi is an online dog food shop. It was designed to allow users to access delicious and nutritious dog food, and easily purchase and get delivered on their door. Easy and quick! Visuals and branding were created to be friendly and modern. The platform was designed with the idea of practice. An easy-to-use platform that provides users with a smooth experience toward helping them making shopping fun and easy.

My Role

For this project, I've been responsible for branding, visuals and app design. That was such a fun project to work with, and I've enjoyed each part of the process.
